BIO 30: Advanced Biology

Course Description

This course offers an advanced study of biology, covering topics such as genetics, ecology, evolution, and human anatomy. It's designed for students with a keen interest in the biological sciences and those pursuing a career in related fields.

Course Objectives

  • Engage with core biological concepts and theories.
  • Develop a critical understanding of biological research and its implications.
  • Apply knowledge to solve complex problems and hypothesize new ideas.
  • Cultivate scientific inquiry and lifelong learning skills.
